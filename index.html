After School Club

About the club

After School Club is a project-based regular meetups where people come along to work on cool and exciting projects. There will be usually 3 or more projects happening at the same time.

Starts on 9th July 2012

Meet ups are on Mondays from 6pm to 22.30pm.

Want to join the club?

If you are interested in becoming a member of the club, let us know by joining our mailing list.
